About

A physician turned public health specialist with 8+ years' experience across operational research, monitoring & evaluation, big data analysis, infectious diseases, field epidemiology, surveillance, prevalence surveys, grants, project management, and digital health—extensively focused on reducing the TB burden and mitigating drug resistance through WHO’s National TB Elimination Program (NTEP).

Dr. Vinod Kumar VG is a physician turned public‑health professional who loves turning messy health data into actionable decisions. He works at the intersection of epidemiology, data analysis, and program operations—designing monitoring frameworks, building feedback loops, and translating insights for field teams and policy makers. He is especially passionate about TB elimination, drug‑resistance surveillance, and leveraging digital health platforms such as Nikshay. Current interests include pragmatic AI for public health (automated dashboards, anomaly detection, geospatial mapping, and natural‑language summaries), privacy‑first data pipelines, and impact evaluation for patient‑support interventions.
